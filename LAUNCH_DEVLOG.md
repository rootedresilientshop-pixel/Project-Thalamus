### 5 Seed Templates (Ready to Load)

To jumpstart your journey, v1.0.0-GOLD includes 5 fully-built, production-ready templates:

1. **Data Pipeline**
   CSV/JSON transformation, filtering, and aggregation workflows. Perfect for data professionals and analysts.

## The Roadmap: Sovereign Modules

Thalamus Core is the chassis. But we're building an ecosystem of sovereign modules that extend it.

### Kanon Sentry Kernel

**Sovereign governance and cryptographic audit logging.**

Kanon adds institutional-grade security to Thalamus deployments:
- Cryptographic audit logging (immutable records of every operation)
- Permission management (fine-grained control over who can do what)
- Local governance framework (transparent, auditable rules)
- Zero cloud dependency (all governance stays local)

**Use case**: Organizations that need compliance, audit trails, and governance without cloud intermediaries.

**Status**: In active development
**GitHub**: https://github.com/dreamcraftstudio/kanon-sentry-kernel

### BridgeMod Hardware Bridge

**Local-first SDK for Raspberry Pi, Arduino, and IoT orchestration.**

BridgeMod turns Thalamus into a hardware control platform:
- Raspberry Pi orchestration (deploy and manage Pi clusters)
- Arduino bridging (connect, program, and monitor microcontrollers)
- Real-time sensor integration (temperature, humidity, motion, etc.)
- Local hardware abstraction layer (no cloud APIs required)

**Use case**: Makers, hardware developers, and IoT enthusiasts who want to control physical devices without vendor-specific platforms or cloud dependencies.

**Status**: In active development
**GitHub**: https://github.com/dreamcraftstudio/bridgemod-hardware-bridge
